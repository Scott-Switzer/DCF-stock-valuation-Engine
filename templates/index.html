Standard DCF Limitations

We exclude assets that require specialized valuation models:

  • ETFs/Funds: No direct cash flows.
  • REITs: Valued using FFO/AFFO.
  • Banks: Valued using Dividends/Residual Income.
  • LPs/MLPs: Different tax/cash structures.
